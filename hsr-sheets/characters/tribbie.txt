NAME: TRIBBIE
RARITY: 5
PATH: harmony

MAIN ROLE: Support + Sub-DPS
BUFF + DEBUFF TYPE: Res-Pen, Vulnerability
DAMAGE TYPE: Additional Damage, FUA
TEAM ARCHETYPES: Hypercarry, Dual DPS, FUA
SP USAGE: SP positive
SKILL ROTATION: EAA

=== GENERAL ANALYSIS ===
Kit Overview: Generalist Team-Wide Support with Rare buffs and DPS abilities.
Tribbie's Skill grant RES PEN, when other allies attack, regenerate energy for Tribbie, when they use Ultimate, Tribbie deal an AoE FUA, with a limit that can be reset after using Ultimate.

Tribbie gain energy whenever an ally hit enemies, the more enemies are hit, the more energy they gain.

Tribbie's Ultimate has the following effect: Launches an AoE attack, creates a [Zone] which inflict Vulnerability to all enemies, after an ally attacks, for every 1 target hit, deals 1 instance of Additional DMG to the target that has the highest HP among the hit targets.

Synergies:
✦  Allies that can frequently attack multiple targets to charge Tribbie's energy and deal more DMG.
✦  Allies that enjoy a support unit who can also attack multiple targets often.
✦  Teams that prefer having Res-Pen and Vulnerability for DMG amplification.
Notes:
✦  Since Tribbie's energy depend on how often and how many targets their allies attack, Tribbie's [Zone] can run into uptime issue in unfavorable situations.
Tips:
✦ Tribbie's potential for Ultimate spam makes them a good holder of Dance!Dance!Dance! Light cone if you can utilize it.

=== WORTH PULLING FOR ===
✦ If you like the character, get them!

✦ Tribbie are another universal Team-wide support unit that offer good and easy to manage buff, making them a good flex slot in most, if not all teams.

✦ Their main niche is for Blast/AoE teams, increasing the ST DMG output in multiple targets scenarios, when in a ST centric team or a low enemies count fight, Tribbie might not seem outstanding,

✦ If you aren't interested in this niche and already have many flex support units, Tribbie can be on lower priority.

=== VERTICAL INVESTMENT EXPLANATION ===
E1: If the [Zone] is active, when allies attack enemies, they additionally deal 24% True DMG of the total DMG to the target with highest HP, a great increase to ST DMG output of the whole team.

E2: [Zone] deal 1 more instance of DMG per trigger and Increase the multiplier of Additional DMG by 120%.

S1 increase wearer's Crit DMG% by 36%, when wearer deals a FUA, regenerates 12 energy and buff team's Crit DMG by 48%, good for DPS Tribbie.

E1S0 Tribbie is great for vertical invest.
