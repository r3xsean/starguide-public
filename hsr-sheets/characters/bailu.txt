=== GENERAL ANALYSIS ===
Kit Overview: Generic universal sustain
Upon using Ultimate, Bailu heals all ally and grant them Invigoration. Invigorated allies will heal whenever they take damage and gain DMG reduction, this effect last for 2 turns.

Bailu's Skill heals 2 randoms allies after healing the targeted ally once. Subsequent heals are less effective after each instances.

When an ally take fatal damage, Bailu can revive them. This can occur only once per battle.

Team Synergies:
✦ Solid sustainer for any teams that need one.

Notes:
✦ Bailu's majority of healings are from her Ultimate and Invigoration, so she rarely has to use Skill. Only use her Skill when necessary.
✦ Bailu does not have any way to counter debuffs and Crowd Control, which makes her a lot less effective when you need a way to deal with Crowd Control.

=== WORTH PULLING FOR ===
✦ If you like the character, get them!

✦ Bailu can be obtained from Standard banner or from losing 50/50 so you do not need to specifically aim to get her.

✦ Bailu is a perfectly serviceable sustain who can do her job well in most scenarios, only struggling in debuff heavy battles due to her lack of Cleanse.
