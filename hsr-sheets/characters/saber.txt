=== GENERAL ANALYSIS ===
Kit Overview: High Energy Crit DPS
Saber's kit revolves around her [Core Resonance]. 1 [Core Resonance] is considered 8 fixed energy. Saber obtains [Core Resonance] through her Skill (+3) / Enhanced Basic Attack (+2) / Ally Ultimate (+3) / Entering battle with Technique (+3). Saber has  can hold 120 excess Energy.

Upon using Saber's Ultimate, she deals 1 instance of AoE damage, then 10 additional bounce damage which also enhances her Basic Attack.

Saber's Enhanced Basic Attack deals AoE damage, Saber will deals more damage if there are less than 2 enemies. Additionally, this ability will grant Mana Burst upon use. Under Mana Burst effect, when Saber's Ultimate can fully be charged with [Core Resonance] and Skill, Saber consumes Mana Burst to immediately take action and gain 1 SP.

Saber has tremendous amount of DMG%, CDMG% and CR% self-buff.

Synergies:
✦ Supports with good damage amplification and action advance.
✦ Supports that can generate Saber's Energy.

Tips:
✦ Conserve ally Ultimates to properly time Mana Burst consumption, especially characters who can generate energy with it.

=== WORTH PULLING FOR ===
✦ If you like the character, get them!

✦ Saber is a strong all-rounded DPS, while being slightly more difficult to pilot with the various buffs she needs to keep track of and how they affect parts of her kit.

✦ Saber has tons of in battle self buffs making her much easier to build around, and make her work with various supports.
