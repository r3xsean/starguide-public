=== GENERAL ANALYSIS ===
Kit Overview: Single-Target FuA Specialist
When Dr. Ratio uses his Skill to deal damage and apply a debuff, there is a chance of launching a FUA attack afterward. The more debuffs the enemy has, the higher the chance to launch a FUA attack and the higher damage they will take from Dr. Ratio. If the enemy die before the FUA attack, he will attack a random enemy instead.

Dr. Ratio's Ultimate deal Single-target damage to an enemy and apply Wiseman's Folly. If this enemy is attacked by ally, Dr. Ratio will launch a FUA attack, this trigger up to 2 times.

Synergies:
✦ Debuffers with constant uptime are essential for consistently triggering FUA attacks.
✦ Supports with strong damage amplification, action advance, energy are highly appreciated.

Notes:
✦ Light Cones that provide debuffs can be valuable on supports and sub-DPSes.

=== WORTH PULLING FOR ===
✦ If you like the character, get them!

✦ Dr. Ratio is a very strong option if help is needed with low-target content like MoC and AS.

✦ He also has several flexible, inexpensive team options that include 4-star supports, making him simple to use even for an account with few 5-stars.

=== E0S0 VIABILITY ===
Yes.

Works in standard Dual DPS and Hypercarry setups, but wants at least 3 consistent debuffs active.

=== VERTICAL INVESTMENT ORDER ===
S1 > E1

=== VERTICAL INVESTMENT EXPLANATION ===
His signature Light Cone is strong, though its value can be diminished in teams with no Def Shred. He also has easy access to a budget option from Herta's Store.

His E1 provides decent damage, but is simply a statstick.

Topaz's signature Light Cone may be considered higher priority for his team if it lacks a third debuff.

=== EXAMPLE TEAMS ===
Premium (Standard): Dr. Ratio / Cipher / Robin / Aventurine
Half Premium: Dr. Ratio / Sparkle / Pela / Sustain
Budget Dual DPS: Dr. Ratio / Moze / Tingyun / Sustain
RRAT (Requires Vertical Investment): Dr. Ratio / Topaz / Robin / Aventurine
Vertical investment is required to ensure 3+ debuffs. Without at least 2 of: Topaz E1; Topaz  S1; Aventurine E2; and Aventurine S1, Topaz or Robin must be replaced with a debuffer.
