=== GENERAL ANALYSIS ===
Kit Overview: Strong autostacking field-based Debuffer
- Whenever Jiaoqiu attacks, inflicts stacks of his [Ashen Roast] debuff. This acts as Burn DoT, and at max stacks (5) applies 35% Vulnerability.

- His Ultimate creates a field, which will cause enemies to take 15% more Ultimate DMG. It also inflicts stacks of [Ashen Roast] at the start of their turns, up to 6 times per Ultimate use.

Synergies:
- Units that want Debuffs, whether it is due to kit requirements or already having strong self-buffs.
- Ultimate-Damage focused DPS benefit highly from his bonus Ultimate Vulnerability.
- Vulnerability amplifies all damage types, including Break; as such, he can be used in all teams.

Tips:
- Against waves of weak mobs, such as in Pure Fiction, Solitary Healing can be his preferred Light Cone for Ultimate spamming.

=== WORTH PULLING FOR ===
- If you like the character, pull them!

- Jiaoqiu is an extremely high priority investment for Acheron teams, where he is consistently Best-in-Slot and a massive upgrade over budget options.

- He has no downtime on his debuffs. As such, he is a strong, consistent AoE debuffer.

- He can fit into all teams, making him a useful backup option for in-demand supports even when not BiS.

=== E0S0 VIABILITY ===
Yes.

He fits anywhere, preferably debuff-based and Ultimate-DMG Teams

=== VERTICAL INVESTMENT ORDER ===
S1 > E1 > E2

=== VERTICAL INVESTMENT EXPLANATION ===
S1 provides a significant 60% EHR buff and a bonus 24% vulnerability, making him easier to build and a stronger debuffer. It can also be used on other Nihility support units.

E1 provides a 40% damage boost to the team, though its faster stacking effect is mostly irrelevant.

E2 is a strong option for DoT teams as his DoT scaling is now comparable to Black Swan's.

Prioritize E2 if wanting to focus on DoT
