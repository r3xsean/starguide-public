SKILL ROTATION: AAA, E when needed

=== GENERAL ANALYSIS ===
Kit Overview: Consistent debuffer and flexible Sub-DPS
When Cipher is present, applies 40% Vulnerability to all enemies and Patron to the enemy with highest HP, when any ally hit this enemy, she launches a FUA attack. Cipher's Skill weakens enemies, when Cipher uses her Ultimate or Skill, she can re-apply Patron on other enemies.

Cipher's Ultimate explanation, notes and tips:
Cipher records the DMG dealt by all allies, excluding True DMG, indicated as coins (1 coin = 1k DMG). Unleash this DMG by using Ultimate and consume all coins, if the attack only has 1 target, all DMG will be directed toward it rather than being distributed unless there are 2 or 3 enemies. If facing elite and boss targets, try to eliminate all mobs target before using her Ultimate. If the boss is AoE or in Pure Fiction, you can spam her Ultimate.

When reaching a certain amount of SPD, she gains CR% and increases the portion of tallied damage. Up to a maximum of 24% on Patron enemy and 16% on other enemies.

Synergies:
- Units who appreciate Cipher's frequent attacks, debuffs application and DMG amplification.
Notes:
- Cipher's multipliers are very low and the majority of her damage is from tallied damage. This allows you to completely neglect her offensive stats.
Tips:
- Cipher's skill is not high priority, use it when there is excess SP.

=== WORTH PULLING FOR ===
- If you like the character, get them!

- Cipher is an extremely consistent and strong debuffer, which makes her a great generalist support to have for any team.

=== E0S0 VIABILITY ===
Yes.

=== VERTICAL INVESTMENT ORDER ===
S1 > E1

=== VERTICAL INVESTMENT EXPLANATION ===
S1 provide SPD, whenever the wearer attacks, they reduces all enemies' DEF. If the wearer have 170 SPD, they reduce additional DEF.

E1 allows Cipher to record 36% non True Damage on Patron enemies and 24% on other enemies. When she unleashes her FUA, she gains 80% ATK.

Since Cipher's damage is already low, she prefers S1 over E1 as it provide ease of building and allows her to amplify her team damage even more.
