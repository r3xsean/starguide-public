=== GENERAL ANALYSIS ===
Kit Overview: Double the skills
✦ Cerydra selects one ally to receive Military Merit. The ally and Cerydra gain a flat SPD buff, and the ally also gets an ATK buff. When the ally deals damage, Cerydra will deal an instance of Additional DMG and gain 5 energy. This can activate up to 20 times, and the trigger count resets when using her Ultimate.

Cerydra gains a point of [Charge] when the ally with Military Merit takes action (+1), and when Cerydra uses her Skill (+1) or Ultimate (+2).
Once reaching 6 points of [Charge], Military Merit upgrades to Peerage, the ally additionally gaining a Skill CRIT DMG and RES PEN buff, consuming 6 points of [Charge] and copies the skill performed by the ally with Military Merit. When used, Peerage will revert back to Military Merit.

Cerydra gives herself a 100% CRIT Rate and massive CRIT DMG buffs.

Synergies:
✦ DPS who primarily deal Skill DMG and can take action often.
Notes:
✦ The skill copied must deal damage to trigger.
✦ If charge is not at max, the ally with Military Merit can grant a stack through their ultimate once per battle.

=== WORTH PULLING FOR ===
✦ If you like the character, get them!

✦ Cerydra is a fairly strong support, however currently she struggles to find use outside of a very select few due to her buffs and how they work. Majority of other DPS would prefer many other options before even considering Cerydra.

=== E0S0 VIABILITY ===
Yes

Cerydra can only be used with DPS who primarily deal Skill DMG

We reccomend you only use her in the teams currently listed.

=== VERTICAL INVESTMENT ORDER ===
E1(For Phainon) > S1 > E1> E2

=== VERTICAL INVESTMENT EXPLANATION ===
S1 helps reach her high ATK goal for her personal DMG, and enhances her utility, restoring SP on ultimate and buffing DMG dealt by an ally's skill

E1 provides an ally DEF Ignore, and restores their energy by 2 when Cerydra uses her skill

E2 increases both her and an ally's DMG dealt

Phainon specifically prefers her E1 because he'll be able to get 2 Coreflames per Cerydra skill, speeding up his ultimate recovery.
