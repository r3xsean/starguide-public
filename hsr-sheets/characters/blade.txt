SKILL ROTATION: E spam until the game tell you to use A

=== GENERAL ANALYSIS (POST 3.4 BUFF) ===
Kit Overview: HP scaling DPS who spam abilities.
When Blade uses his skill, he consumes his HP and enters [Hellscape] state. In this state, he deals more damage; increase the chance of getting hit; and enhances his Basic Attack, which will consume his HP when used and deals Blast damage. His Enhanced Basic Attack will not generate SP and he will exit [Hellscape] state after 3 turns.

When Blade's HP reduces or he takes damage, he gains 1 Charge, when he obtain 5 Charge, Blade unleashes an AoE FUA attack, gains 15 energy and heals himself.

When Blade uses his Ultimate, he sets his HP to 50% of his max HP and deals Blast damage based on his max HP and the tallied HP that he lost during the battle.

Synergies:
✦ Blade works well with most Nihility/Harmony units as long as they give good damage amplification, action advance, etc.
✦ Prefers supports who can drain his HP for more charge and can synergize with units who can drain allies HP (Usually not recommended).

Notes:
✦ Blade can go slow or fast, depending on what supports you're using.

=== WORTH PULLING FOR ===
✦ If you like the character, get them!

✦ Blade is a very consistent and good crit DPS, he can works for most content.

✦ However, Blade also shares the same supports/teams with quite a few units which can cause characters overlap.

=== E0S0 VIABILITY ===
Yes.
His damage scales on his HP now, instead of being mixed like pre-buff. All generalist supports still work.
Put Blade in the middle spot in case you get lucky from getting hit with Blast attacks

=== VERTICAL INVESTMENT ORDER ===
E1>S1

=== VERTICAL INVESTMENT EXPLANATION ===
E1 allows Blade's Enhanced Basic Attack and Ultimate to deal additional damage based on 150% of his lost tallied HP.

S1 is a good stat stick with decent effects for Blade, however he has great alternative options.
