=== GENERAL ANALYSIS ===
Kit overview: Single-target FUA Sub-DPS
Topaz randomly inflicts [Proof of Debt] debuff on 1 target which increases Follow-Up Attack DMG taken, using Skill can reapply this debuff and let Numby attacks once.

Numby appears on the action order and attack the target with said debuff, it gets advanced by 50% if its target is attacked by a FUA attack. All Topaz's attacks are considered FUA.

Topaz's Ultimate buffs Numby and it can be advanced by any attacks of other teammates for 2 turns.

Synergies:
✦ Topaz works well with any FUA DPS.
✦ Support units that can amplify team DMG, as Topaz damage contribution to the team is significant.
✦ Units that has frequent FUA attacks.

Notes:
✦ Topaz can be a SP neutral or SP positive sub-DPS as spamming skill is unnecessary.
✦ She can even be played as a hypercarry role but require good investment and supports to have decent results. Otherwise, using her in a sub-DPS role is recommended.

=== WORTH PULLING FOR ===
✦ If you like the character, get them!

✦ Topaz is a good ally option for most FUA DPS, but since she doesn't offer other specialized effects and her debuff is single target, she isn't high priority pull for most FUA DPS.
