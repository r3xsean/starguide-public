NAME: FUGUE
RARITY: 5
PATH: Nihility

MAIN ROLE: Break-Super Break + Debuffer Support
BUFF TYPE: Break Effect%, Super Break enabler
DEBUFF TYPE: Delay, DEF Shred, Exo-Toughness
TEAM ARCHETYPES: Break, Super Break, Debuff
SP USAGE: SP Positive
SKILL ROTATION: EAA, or E to switch target

=== GENERAL ANALYSIS ===
Kit overview: Premium Super Break Support
Just by existing in the battle, Fugue allows all allies to deal Super Break DMG against weakness broken enemies and inflicts all enemies with [Cloudflame Luster], which is an additional Toughness you can break, triggering corresponding Break DMG and Weakness Break effect once broken.

Fugue's skill grants 1 ally [Foxian's Prayer] effect: Increases Break Effect%, attacks from this ally can deal universal Toughness Reduction with 50% reduced value and are able to inflict Defense shred. Lastly, enhances Basic Attack, making it Blast type to contribute more Toughness Reduction.

Enemy target will get delayed by 15% each time its Toughness is broken, allowing more chances to deal Super Break DMG

Synergies:
- Super Break or Break DPS and units with high Toughness Reduction in general.
- Units that benefit from breaking enemies' Toughness and/or debuffs.

Notes:
- Fugue's ultimate is an attack that can ignore Weakness Type, with no further effect.
- Her universal Toughness Reduction effect can not stack with other sources (ie: Rappa's EBA)

Tips:
- Use Fugue's skill on ally capable of high Toughness Reduction (ie: Lingsha, Harmony TB) to help with Toughness damage then swap back to the main DPS unit once targets are (close to) broken.

=== WORTH PULLING FOR ===
- If you like the character, get them!

- Getting Fugue would let you have a good to great upgrade to Harmony Trailblazer, or a replacement in case you use Remembrance Trailblazer elsewhere

- If you have or intend to have multiple Break-based teams, pulling for Fugue is also recommended.

=== E0S0 VIABILITY ===
Yes

Best with Break teams, with some niche situation to fit in

=== VERTICAL INVESTMENT ORDER ===
E1 > S1 > E2

=== VERTICAL INVESTMENT EXPLANATION ===
E1 lets ally with [Foxian Prayer] increases their Weakness Break Efficiency by 50%, crucial for breaking and Super Break DMG.

S1 increases BE%, helps with maxing out Fugue's team BE% buff and offers Break DMG vulnerability.

E2 gives energy when breaking an enemy, Fugue's Ultimate now Advances Forward the whole team by 24%, which can be hard to utilize for more casual players.

E1S0 is a fine stop.
