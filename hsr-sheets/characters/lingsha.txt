=== GENERAL ANALYSIS ===
Kit Overview: Constant team-wide Healer and Area of Effect Attacker
Lingsha summons [Fuyuan] on the Action Order with her skill and technique.

Lingsha's Skill deals AoE damage, heal allies, advance [Fuyuan] action, and grant 3 action count, up to 5 maximum. If an ally gets below 60% HP, [Fuyuan] launches an attack. The effect triggers per 2 turns

When [Fuyuan] take action, it will consume 1 action count to deal AoE damage (This is considered FUA), cleanse one debuff and heal all allies.

Finally, her Ultimate inflicts Break Damage Vulnerability and Advance Forward [Fuyuan] by 100%.
Team Synergies:
✦ Characters that benefit from the many attacks of Lingsha: (AoE attack, Fire Toughness DMG, FUA).
✦ Break and Super Break teams and units.
Note:
✦ In some rare cases, [Fuyuan] might arrive sooner or later than when you need it.
Tips:
✦ You can use Basic Attack for the first 3 of Lingsha's turns after using her technique.
✦ Purposely lowering your units' HP/DEF, making them more squishy to trigger [Fuyuan]'s emergency heal early for more AoE attacks is an option.

=== WORTH PULLING FOR ===
✦ If you like the character, get them!

✦ Being a Limited 5-star sustain, Lingsha is a worthwhile pick up for anyone seeking comfort while playing.

✦ Her abundant streams of FUA, AoE attacks and Break Damage Vulnerability can be helpful for various team setups.

=== VERTICAL INVESTMENT ORDER ===
E1 = S1 = Luocha S1

=== VERTICAL INVESTMENT EXPLANATION ===
Lingsha's AoE attacks synergize well with Luocha's Sig, giving her more energy to spam Ultimate for even more AOE attacks.

E1 boosts self Weakness-break efficiency. Reduces 20% DEF of weakness-broken enemies.

S1 buffs BE% and wearer's Ult attack inflicts 10% Vulnerability, 18% if wearer has 150+ BE%. Can be used by other units, increases Lingsha's DMG overall.

There are multiple choices to vertically invest, so it's up to one's preference.
