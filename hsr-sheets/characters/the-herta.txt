=== GENERAL ANALYSIS ===
Kit Overview: AoE Nuke DPS with Ultimate and Enhanced Skill
When The Herta joins the battle, enemies hit by allies get inflicted with 1 stack of [Interpretation], up to 42 stacks. For each enemy attacked, generates 3 fixed energy for The Herta. Each stack of [Interpretation], along with the effects she provides pairing with another Erudition ally, give her numerous self buffs to tackle a variety of battles.

Her Ultimate rearranges stacks of [Interpretation] and unleashes an attack, Advances Forward her by 100%, and enhances her next Skill. Her Enhanced Skill deals devastating damage and resets [Interpretation] stacks of the main target to 1.

Synergies:
✦ Erudition ally is a must to activate her team buff Trace and help stack [Interpretation], along with units that have frequent AoE damage instances.
✦ Supports that buff The Herta and her team's damage and energy, or provide Speed buffs/Action Advance to improve action frequency.
Notes:
✦ Since [Interpretation] stacks & The Herta's energy gained is based on the amount of enemies and how often they get attacked, her damage output is lowered in 1-3 targets fights.
✦ The Herta's teams can be Skill Point intensive, prioritize giving her SP to use skill.
Tips:
✦ Erudition allies can use equipments such as ERR% rope, 5% ERR% planar ornaments and the Passkey Light Cone to attack more often and recharge her energy.

=== WORTH PULLING FOR ===
✦ If you like the character, get them!

✦ The Herta can deal a great amount of damage in all kind of situations, provided she can consistently build up [Interpretation] stacks. (5-target linked boss, boss with summons, Pure Fiction, etc.)

✦ The Herta has worse performance in pure ST fights.

✦ If wanting a reliable AoE DPS, The Herta would be a great pickup.

✦ Finally, her Technique provides great Quality-of-Life by clearing waves of enemies in Simulated Universe and detecting chests. It's useful for traversing both in SU and the overworld.

=== E0S0 VIABILITY ===
Yes

Allies that can attack multiple enemies often should be priority.
Erudition ally changes based on the enemies.
Both Team-wide and Hypercarry support can be used.

=== VERTICAL INVESTMENT ORDER ===
E2 > E1 >= S1

=== VERTICAL INVESTMENT EXPLANATION ===
E1 increases Enhanced Skill DMG and reduces the time needed to stack up [Interpretation].

E2 makes Enhanced Skill Advance Forward The Herta by 35% and allows for a second Enhanced Skill per Ult, greatly improving the frequency of her explosive damage output.

S1 provides QoL for SP economy and a good DMG buff but there are many alternatives for Erudition LC.

The Herta E0S0 is solid, E2S0/S1 is great for spenders.
