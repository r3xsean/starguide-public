NAME: RAPPA
RARITY: 5
PATH: Erudition

MAIN ROLE: DPS
TYPE: Break, Super Break
TEAM ARCHETYPES: Break, Super Break
SP USAGE: SP Negative, Less frequent SP usage than average DPS
SKILL ROTATION: E spam until the game tell you to use A

=== GENERAL ANALYSIS ===
Kit Overview: Constant AoE Super Break DPS
Rappa's main focus is her Ultimate: enters [Sealform] state, enhances Basic Attack to deal Universal Toughness Reduction to all enemies (though attacking non Imaginary-weak only deals half the Toughness Reduction), and allows her to deal Super Break DMG once enemies are broken.

When allies Weakness Break an enemy, her talent gains a charge (store up to 10), which enables her to deal huge Break DMG, even in ST scenarios.

Synergies:
✦ Units that has Break-Damage related buff: Super Break enabler, WBE buff, DEF shred, etc.
✦ Supports and DPS units that helps with breaking enemies.

Notes:
✦ Rappa struggles against enemies immune to Toughness Reduction.
✦ Since Rappa does not implant Weakness, she would have a slower clear time if enemies' weakness don't match with Rappa or other teammates.

Tips:
✦ The first 2 hits of EBA is Blast, manage it well to distribute your Toughness Reduction more evenly.
✦ Imaginary Break DMG has a low multiplier, so allies of other types can handle breaking instead.

=== WORTH PULLING FOR ===
✦ If you like the character, get them!

✦ Rappa is bound to perform with low investment as long as she or her team can Weakness Break enemies, mainly in AOE fights.

✦ Prime example being Pure Fiction, where enemies abundant and have reduced Toughness value, or when bosses summon more enemies on the fields.

✦ In unfavorable situations, Rappa would require more vertical investment to clear comfortably.

=== E0S0 VIABILITY ===
Yes.

Fugue / Dahlia is nearly fixed for more Super Break DMG proc.
Allies that can contribute to breaking are also beneficial for the battle.

=== VERTICAL INVESTMENT ORDER ===
Fugue > Dahlia > S1 > E1 > E2

=== VERTICAL INVESTMENT EXPLANATION ===
Fugue's Exo-Toughness is highly appreciated by Rappa. Dahlia also provides huge amount of DMG increase, as well as weakness implant.

S1 grants energy to use Ultimate right away, gives AA% per 2 EBA, which boost her turn frequency greatly, making S1 is a strong priority.

E1 gives DEF ignore and lets you keep Ultimate uptime more comfortably, E2 increases your ST Toughness damage.

E0S1 Rappa is a good stop, afterwards, you should focus on her supports.
