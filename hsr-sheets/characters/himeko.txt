=== GENERAL ANALYSIS ===
Kit Overview: FUA-orientated AoE DPS
Himeko's Skill does Blast damage, and deals more damage if the enemy is afflicted with Burn. Every attack of hers has a chance to inflict Burn.
Her Ultimate deals AoE damage to all enemies, and more energy is refunded to this Ult if any enemy is defeated.

The main premise of Himeko is her Talent. This Talent tallies up to 3 Charges, gains 1 when the battle starts, 1 when a common enemy has its Weakness Broken, and 3 if an Elite or Boss gets Weakness Broken.
Upon obtaining 3 Charges, Himeko launches a FUA that deals AoE damage, and then clears all Charges.

Synergies:
✦ Most of Himeko's damage comes from her Talent, allies that can assist with breaking faster/obtain more breaks is invaluable.
✦ Her AoE-damage FUA attack makes Himeko a good driver for other characters such as Herta, Jade, etc.
Notes:
✦ Try to manage Himeko's Charge to avoid wasting Charges since she can only hold 3 Charges. In battle, try to break Elite/Boss first.
✦ Himeko's damage against a single target is pitiful, she's mostly good for AoE situations such as Pure Fiction.
