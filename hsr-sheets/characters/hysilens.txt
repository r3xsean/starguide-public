NAME: HYSILENS
RARITY: 5
PATH: Nihility

MAIN ROLE: DPS/Support
TYPE: DoT
TEAM ARCHETYPES: DoT, Debuff
SP USAGE: SP positive
SKILL ROTATION: EAA, EAE if needed

=== GENERAL ANALYSIS ===
Kit Overview: Damage over Time DPS
Whenever any ally attacks, Hysilens can inflict a DoT type to the enemy, up to 4 types of DoT.

Hysilens' Skill inflict Vulnerability to all enemy.

When unleashing Ultimate, Hysilens grants 1 SP, deploys a zone and detonates all the DoT on the enemies. In this zone, enemies' ATK and DEF are reduced; when enemy takes damage from a DoT type, Hysilens deals extra Bleed DoT to them and she can only deal 8 extra Bleed DoT max in this way, This zone last for 3 turns.

Synergies:
- DoT detonators and other DoT DPS that benefits from her multiple DoT types.
- Teamwide buffer, AoE debuffers, etc.

Notes:
- Make sure to manage SP effectively, as DoT teams are very SP hungry in general

=== WORTH PULLING FOR ===
- If you like a character, get them!

- Hysilens is a very important teammate for DoT archetype, it is recommended to get her if you plan to play DoT.

- Hysilens has decent general debuffing capabilities that can be used in niche situations. However it is still recommended that she be primarily pulled for her main role as a DoT DPS instead of a debuffing support.

=== E0S0 VIABILITY ===
Yes, but with Kafka

Kafka is nearly mandatory for Hysilens to function well, as for the other member slot, another DoT DPS or damage amplifier of any sorts are highly appreciated.

=== VERTICAL INVESTMENT ORDER ===
Kafka > E1 > S1

=== VERTICAL INVESTMENT EXPLANATION ===
Even though Hysilens can occasionally detonate DoT via Ultimate, Kafka is still a requirement for DoT teams as she's the only consistent DoT detonator available.

E1 increases team's DoT damage, and allows Hysilens to inflict an additional DoT whenever ally attacks, up to 8 types of DoT on enemies.

S1 increases the wearer EHR% and grants SPD to allies whenever they attack enemies. When the wearer inflict debuffs, they can inflict DoT Vulnerability.
