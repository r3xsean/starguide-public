=== GENERAL ANALYSIS ===
Kit Overview: Crit DPS who transform into a boss
Phainon's mechanics are based around obtaining Coreflames. Methods of getting Coreflames: Being targeted by allies' or enemies' abilities or enter battles (+1) / Getting energy restored by allies' ability (+1) / Using Skill (+2) / Leaving Territory (+3).

Phainon can hold up to 15 Coreflames, and requires 12 to cast his Ultimate. After casting Ultimate, he gains 8 extra turns and enters a special Territory where all teammates become Departed (removed temporarily from the team).

In Phainon's Ultimate, he uses a resource named Scourge which replaces the function of Skill Points. He has 3 abilities in his Ultimate form :
- [Basic Attack]: Blast attack, restores 2 Scourge,
- [Skill 1]: Makes all enemies move instantly to attack him, gains Scourge equal to the number of enemies on field, and then Counter with an AoE + Bounce attack. The counter's damage increases the more Phainon is hit.
- [Skill 2]: Consumes 1-4 Scourge to deal AoE + Bounce damage, with a big bonus multiplier when consuming 4.
On his 8th extra turn, Phainon will cast a last AoE attack and exits his Territory, returning his teammates to the field.

Synergies:
✦ Harmony units who can generate Coreflame, provide action advance, energy, and good damage amplification.
Notes:
✦ Since all allies Depart in Territory, Phainon lacks flexibility in terms of gameplay and has to rely more on his stats/investments.
✦ Phainon's team in general is very Skill Point hungry without investments such as S1 Sunday, etc.
✦ It is generally not recommended to use Phainon with a Sustainer due to how his Territory works.
Tips:
✦ If Phainon's Coreflame generation is ensured, strong damage amplification options such as Tribbie or Robin can be used.

=== WORTH PULLING FOR ===
✦ If you like a character, get them!

✦ Phainon is a great hypercarry DPS who can clear most content at ease with good investments and does not require too much skill to pilot correctly.

✦ Phainon relies on his stats and investments more than most characters in the game. As he lacks gameplay flexibility to compensate for a lack of investment or bad relic quality.

=== E0S0 VIABILITY ===
Yes

Phainon's team may struggle with Skill Points without proper management.

=== VERTICAL INVESTMENT ORDER ===
Sunday and Cerydra > E2 > S1 > E1

=== VERTICAL INVESTMENT EXPLANATION ===
Sunday and Cerydra are very important since they can generate Coreflame stacks, provide great buffs, and action advance for Phainon.

E2 increases Physical RES PEN, and when Phainon uses [Skill 2] with 4 Scourge, he gains an extra turn. This effectively extends his transformation and damage.

E1 increases CDMG%, and defeating enemies during transformation will increase his SPD in Territory.

S1 increases base SPD and allows attacks to ignore DEF. When the wearer uses their Ultimate they get a damage buff that is removed at the start of their turn.
