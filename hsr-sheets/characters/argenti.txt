=== GENERAL ANALYSIS ===
Kit Overview: AoE specialized DPS
Argenti's Talent grant him energy whenever he attacks, the more enemies he hits, the more energy he gains. Each enemies hit will also grant him [Apotheosis] stacks, which increases his Crit Rate and Crit Damage.

Argenti has 180 max Energy. Argenti's Skill and Ultimate deals AoE damage to enemies. However his ultimate has 2 forms:
- [For In This Garden Supreme Beauty Bestows]: Consumes 90 energy to unleash an AoE attack
- [Merit Bestowed in "My" Garden]: Consumes 180 energy to unleash an even a stronger AoE attack. He will also deals 6 additional instances of bounce damage afterwards.

Synergies:
✦ Supports that provide action advance, energy or good damage amplification
✦ Work as enabler for other erudition unit, such as with Jade, Mini Herta or The Herta.
Notes:
✦ Argenti's scaling on his fully charged Ultimate is pretty low, it's recommended to spam his 90 cost Ultimate for finishing off minions or small mobs.
✦ ERR% rope can be used on Pure Fiction build

=== WORTH PULLING FOR ===
✦ If you like the character, pull them!

✦ Argenti has always been a great DPS option in Pure Fiction, he is a great pull if you lack good DPS for this specific content. However his performance in other content may not be as great due to mediocre scalings.
