NAME: JADE
RARITY: 5
PATH: Erudition

MAIN ROLE: DPS
TYPE: Crit
TEAM ARCHETYPES: FUA, Dual DPS, Hypercarry (uncommon)
SP USAGE: SP Positive
SKILL ROTATION: EAA

=== GENERAL ANALYSIS ===
Kit Overview: Powerful Area of Effect specialist.
Jade's Skill applies [Debt Collector] to an ally, granting 30 Speed and Additional Damage with no downtime at the cost of 2% of that ally's HP per their attack.

AoE FUA is launched at 8 Charges, which are gained per enemy hit by her and her [Debt Collector]'s attacks. Jade's FUAs do not count towards these Charges. Her Talent grants massive self-buffs, making her less reliant on supports. Ultimate deals respectable damage and enhances her next 2 FUAs.

Synergies:
✦ Frequent multi-target attacker to be made as the [Debt Collector].
✦ Support units who can buff the whole team, or ones who help generate Charges by Advancing her [Debt Collector].
✦Characters that value the HP drains

Note:
✦ Jade's performance is rather subpar in 1 or 2 targets situation.

Tips:
✦ Hypercarry Jade's [Debt Collector] should still be a unit with frequent AoE attacks, as the amount of Charges Jade generates on her own turn is low.

=== WORTH PULLING FOR ===
✦ If you like the character, get them!

✦ As long as Jade can consistently gain her Charge,  she will be able to perform excellently in multi-target scenarios.

✦ More vertical investment might be needed for ST/low-target fights, but at that point, you might want to let other DPS do the job instead.

=== E0S0 VIABILITY ===
Yes.

A very flexible addition to AoE Dual DPS comps.
E1 enables teams with a ST Debt Collector.

=== VERTICAL INVESTMENT ORDER ===
Lingsha >= E1 >=  S1

=== VERTICAL INVESTMENT EXPLANATION ===
E1 Increase FUA's Damage, targeted ally now generates 3 Charges per attack minimum.

Since you normally don't bring Jade to a pure ST fight, Lingsha being a constant AOE sustain character will be a good [Debt Collector] for her, thus making E1 only worth if you want to explore Jade's viability with ST units or battles.

S1 is a really strong stat boost.

Efficiency wise, Lingsha with E0S1 or E0S0 Jade is a strong combo.
