=== GENERAL ANALYSIS ===
Kit Overview: Hypercarry support unit with potent Action Advances.
Bronya's Skill lets 1 target immediately take action, increases their DMG% and dispels 1 debuff with her Skill, the buff lasting for 1 turn. Her ultimate grants Crit DMG% and ATK for the entire team.
While on the field, Bronya also buffs her team's DMG%.

Her Basic Attack also advances her action forward, making it useful for certain rotations.

Synergies:
✦ Bronya is great for units who highly value action advance and deals majority of their damage on turn.

Notes:
✦ Bronya's kit only buffs her target for low amounts of turns, making her mostly a Hypercarry support. She is not the most suitable for multiple DPS teams.
✦ Bronya without E1 and/or S1/Sunday S1 can be very SP intensive, requires good SP management in order to maximize her potential.
✦ Due to her short buff duration, Bronya should be used with DPS who deals damage on-turn.

Tips:
✦ Bronya can also advance Harmony units, unlike Sunday, so using her to advance Sunday -> your main DPS is a very viable team comp.

=== WORTH PULLING FOR ===
✦ If you like the character, get them!

✦ Bronya can be obtained from Standard banner or from losing 50/50 so you do not need to specifically aim to get her.

=== VERTICAL INVESTMENT EXPLANATION ===
E1 has a 50% chance to regain a SP upon Skill, reset after 1 turn.

E2 provide 30% SPD to the selected target by her Skill for 1 turn.

Bronya S1 provide ERR%, SP when using Ultimate and small damage buff.

Due to how SP hungry Bronya is, E1 and/or S1 are very high value if you use Bronya often, her subsequent eidolons are fine, which can unlock some extra synergy with certain DPS but usually not needed.
