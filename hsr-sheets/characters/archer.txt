=== GENERAL ANALYSIS ===
Kit Overview: CRIT DPS who eats SP whole
Archer's Skill makes him enter the Circuit Connection state and extends his turn. In this state, he consumes 2 SPs to launch a powerful ST attack. This attack is considered Skill damage, Archer will exit this state after 5 Uses / Manually Ending / Run out of SP. Using this ability repeatedly will also greatly increase Skill damage.

In battle, Archer increases the SP cap by 2. Whenever allies attack enemies, Archer consumes 1 Charge to launch an FUA, and gains 1 SP. Archer's Ultimate deals massive ST to an enemy and grants 2 Charge. Additionally, entering battle or using his Technique will also grant 1 Charge.

Synergies:
✦ Supports that can generate Skill Points frequently or do not use much SPs themselves.
✦ Supports with good damage amplification, action advance, etc.

Tips:
✦ Make sure Archer has as many Skill Points as possible before he enters the Circuit Connection state again.

=== WORTH PULLING FOR ===
✦ If you like a character, pull them! Archer will be given out for free during version 3.4, so you are likely to get him regardless.

✦ Archer is a great DPS for low-target content, and he is a great pick if you lack DPS of this archetype. However he will struggle in AoE situations such as Pure Fiction due to the nature of the Hunt path.

✦ Archer's teams are also very flexible, and can work with variety of supports. This makes him quite easy to play.

=== E0S0 VIABILITY ===
Yes.

Archer prefers a hypercarry setup with characters who can generate SPs quickly or do not consume much SP.

=== VERTICAL INVESTMENT ORDER ===
Sparkle E0S0 >>>> E1 > E2

=== VERTICAL INVESTMENT EXPLANATION ===
Sparkle increases the max amount of Skill Points while also generating a lot of SPs in a short burst, which makes her the most important support for Archer.

E1 will refund 2 SPs for 3 Skill attacks, this occur once a turn. This potentially extends Circuit Connection state and increases Archer's damage output.

E2 makes Archer's Ultimate reduce Quantum RES and apply Quantum weakness on the attacked enemy. This is a very solid damage increase.
