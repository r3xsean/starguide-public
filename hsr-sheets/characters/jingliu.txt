=== GENERAL ANALYSIS (POST 3.4 BUFF) ===
Kit Overview: Blast-focused HP Scaling Skill Crit DPS
When Jingliu uses her Skill (ST) or Ultimate (Blast), she gains 1 stack of Syzygy. When she has 2 Syzygy stacks, Jingliu enters [Spectral Transmigration] state, advance herself, gain an extra Syzygy stack and increase its cap to 4 . In this state, Jingliu gains Crit Rate and can only use Enhanced Skill.

When Jingliu uses Enhanced Skill, consumes allies' HP and a stack of Syzygy to launch a Blast attack.

Whenever allies' HP reduce, she gains Moonlight stacks, which increases Crit Damage. Additionally when allies' HP are reduced 20 times, gains 1 Syzygy stack.

Synergies:
✦ Units that can drain allies' HP to help generate Syzygy stacks.
✦ Works well with most Harmony/Nihility that provide damage amplification, action advance, energy, etc.
Notes:
✦ Due to the sheer amount of CDMG% and CR% self-buff, Jingliu prefers supports that provide a different damage amplification such as RES PEN or debuffs. Jingliu prefers HP and potentially SPD more due to this reason
✦ In order to maintain full uptime of Spectral Transmigration state, S1 Hyacine is required.
Tips:
✦ Jingliu should use her Ultimate during [Spectral Transmigration] state to fully benefit from her self-buff. Unless energy is extremely overcapped.
✦ Since Jingliu does not consume Skill Points during [Spectral Transmigration], this allows other allies to spam their skill more often.

=== WORTH PULLING FOR ===
✦ If you like the character, get them!

✦ Jingliu is a very solid crit DPS, who can perform relatively well for most content. She especially excels in 3-enemy situations or even Single Target with E1.

✦ However, Jingliu also shares the same supports/teams with quite a few units which can cause characters overlap.

=== E0S0 VIABILITY ===
Yes.
Jingliu prefers typical hypercarry setup, prioritize teammates that gives action advance and non-saturated buffs or debuffs.

Jingliu also prefers healers over shielders or tankers like Fu xuan.

=== VERTICAL INVESTMENT ORDER ===
Hyacine E0S1 > Mydei S1 > E1

=== VERTICAL INVESTMENT EXPLANATION ===
Hyacine E0S1 is especially important for Jingliu as it allows her to maintain a full uptime on her [Spectral Transmigration] state on top of a solid HP buff.

Mydei S1 gives better damage increase than her own S1 and also contribute to generating Syzygy stacks

E1 increase her CDMG% and Jingliu will deal additional damage to the primary target, This makes Jingliu more potent for pure ST battles.

Hyacine E0S1 should be prioritized before anything else.
