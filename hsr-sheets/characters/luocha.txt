=== GENERAL ANALYSIS ===
Kit overview: Constant team-wide healer
When Luocha uses his Skill or emergency heal activation when an ally's HP drop below 50% or uses his Ultimate, he gains 1 stack of [Abyssal Flower]. When he obtains 2 stacks, he consumes all stacks and unleashes a field which lasts for 2 turns, anyone who attack enemies in this field will heal themselves and the entire party.

Luocha's skill and emergency heal are the same thing, which is a single-target heal and remove 1 debuff on the selected ally.

Luocha's ultimate dispel 1 buff on enemies.

Team Synergies:
✦ Any units who can attack frequently and can constantly heal the team
✦ Units who highly appreciate the amount of SP that Luocha generates
✦ Characters who consumes a large portion of their own HP or other allies' HP

Notes:
✦ Luocha does not need to consume any SP to sustain, so he's always SP positive
✦ In order to maintain a 100% field time, some good timing when using Ultimate is recommended
✦ Due to his absurdly high raw healing capabilities, going for HP/DEF mainstat is also a good choice as Luocha is very squishy because of his ATK scaling heals
