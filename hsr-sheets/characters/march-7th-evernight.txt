=== GENERAL ANALYSIS ===
Kit Overview: HP Manipulating Rememberance Support/DPS
After entering combat or using Skill / Ultimate, Evernight will summon her memosprite, Evey.
Evernight has large self CD, CR, and DMG% buffs, restores extra energy when she or allied Memosprites use abilities, and both she and Evey consume HP when using abilities.

Evernight's skill consumes her HP to increase the CD of ally memosprities in the team by 24% of her own CD

Evernight generates Memoria upon her and memosprite actions, or when losing health.

Evey has two skills:
When Memoria is less than 16:
Evey deals single target damage based on the number of Memoria stacks
When Memoria reaches 16:
Evey action advances itself. Its next attack deals AoE damage based on the amount of Memoria possesed. Afterwards, all Memoria is consumed and Evey self-destructs, restoring one SP and increasing Evernight's speed, scaling on Memoria consumed, until her next turn.

Evernight's ultimate deals AoE damage and grants Evernight two charges of [Darkest Riddle]. While Evernight has charges of [Darkest Riddle], enemies receive increased damage, Evernight and Evey deal more damage, and Evernight gains more Memoria after using her skill. Evey consumes a charge of [Darkest Riddle] after exploding.

Team synergies:
- Characters who consume allies' HP and/or gain benefit from Evernight and Evey's self HP drains.
- Remembrance Characters.
- Support characters to amplify team's DMG.

=== WORTH PULLING FOR ===
- If you like a character, get them!

- Evernight is a solid addition to Castorice's team.

- Evernight also performs well as a hypercarry DPS. However, her teambuilding is very restrictive thanks to her wanting rememberance characters, especially Hyacine, on her team. Therefore, her hypercarry team ends up having a large overlap with other premium teams.

=== E0S0 VIABILITY ===
Yes

Evernight greatly benefits from having several rememberance characters on her team

=== VERTICAL INVESTMENT ORDER ===
E2 > E1 > S1

=== VERTICAL INVESTMENT EXPLANATION ===
E1: increases memosprite damage based on the number of enemies on field. Strong damage increase to memosprite focused teams.

S1: Ally memosprites ignore 20% of enemy DEF, the wearer and their Memosprite's damage increases by 30%, and the wearer gains 8 energy after their Memosprite dies. Solid damage and buff, while allowing quicker Ult rotations for Evernight.

E2: Increases CD, increase Memoria gained by 2 and using Ultimate will provide 2 extra [Darkest Riddle] charges. This massively increases her personal damage output.

S1 is prioritized if there is no good alternative options for her.
